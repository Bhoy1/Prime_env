SENATE AMENDMENT TO THE RESCISSIONS ACT OF 2025; PROVIDING FOR THE PUBLIC RELEASE OF CERTAIN DOCUMENTS, RECORDS, AND COMMUNICATIONS RELATED TO THE INVESTIGATION OF JEFFREY EPSTEIN

Committee on Rules: Full Committee held a hearing on the Senate amendment to H.R. 4, the ``Rescissions Act of 2025''; and legislation on providing for the adoption of the resolution (H. Res. 589) providing for the public release of certain documents, records, and communications related to the investigation of Jeffrey Epstein. The Committee granted, by a record vote of 9–4, a rule providing that the House shall be considered to have taken from the Speaker's table H.R. 4, the ``Rescissions Act of 2025'', with the Senate amendment thereto, and to have concurred in the Senate amendment. The Committee granted, by a record vote of 9–4, a rule providing that House Resolution 589 is hereby adopted. Testimony was heard from Representatives Diaz-Balart and DeLauro.
